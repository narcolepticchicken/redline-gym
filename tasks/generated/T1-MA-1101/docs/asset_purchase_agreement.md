# Asset Purchase Agreement

This Asset Purchase Agreement is entered into by and between Northstar Platform Labs, Inc. ("Buyer") and SignalPeak AI, LLC ("Seller") for Buyer's acquisition of assets used in Seller's SignalDesk business.

## 1. Definitions and Transaction Structure

"Assumed Liabilities" means only the customer support obligations, post-closing service credits, and ordinary vendor obligations expressly listed in Section 5. "Excluded Liabilities" has the meaning stated in Section 4. Buyer and Seller intend a purchase of assets and not a merger, consolidation, or equity acquisition.

## 2. Purchased Assets

At closing, Seller will sell to Buyer all assets used in or necessary for the SignalDesk business, including source code, object code, documentation, product data, domain names, code repositories, assignable intellectual property rights, customer records, assigned contracts, equipment, and goodwill.

## 3. Excluded Assets

Seller retains cash, tax refunds for periods ending before closing, corporate minute books, insurance policies, privileged communications unrelated to the Purchased Assets, and assets expressly listed as excluded on Schedule 3.

## 4. Excluded Liabilities

Seller retains all Excluded Liabilities, including all pre-closing taxes, employment, wage, benefit, discrimination, termination, and similar employee claims, liabilities related to excluded assets, litigation, borrowed money, transaction expenses, and obligations not disclosed in this Agreement or specifically assumed by Buyer.

## 5. Assumed Liabilities

Buyer assumes only the Assumed Liabilities and no other debts, duties, claims, obligations, or liabilities of Seller. The assumed items are limited to customer support obligations arising after closing under assigned customer contracts, post-closing service credits issued by Buyer, and ordinary vendor obligations first arising after closing under assigned contracts.

## 6. Purchase Price and Escrow

The purchase price is $24,000,000. At closing, Buyer will deposit 10% of the purchase price with the escrow agent, and the escrow will remain available for Seller indemnification obligations for 12 months after closing.

## 7. Purchase Price Adjustment

Within 60 days after closing, Buyer will deliver a closing statement showing final working capital against a target of $1,850,000. Buyer's closing statement will be final and binding when delivered, with no separate objection, negotiation, or accountant review process.

## 8. Representations and Warranties

Seller represents that it owns the Purchased Assets free of liens except as stated on Schedule 8(a), has authority to enter into this Agreement, has paid taxes due for pre-closing periods except as stated on Schedule 8(b), and has no undisclosed litigation except as stated on Schedule 8(c). Each representation is qualified only by the specific schedule expressly referenced in that representation.

## 9. Survival and Indemnification

General representations and warranties survive for 12 months after closing, and fundamental representations expire at the same time. Seller will indemnify Buyer for breaches, Excluded Liabilities, and fraud. Seller's indemnification cap is 20% of the purchase price, subject to a true deductible basket of $250,000 that applies only to losses above the deductible amount and does not recover from the first dollar.

## 10. Independent Investigation and Fraud Preservation

Buyer acknowledges that it has conducted an independent investigation and is not relying on statements outside this Agreement, but nothing in this Section 10 limits any claim based on Fraud, intentional misrepresentation, or willful misconduct.

## 11. Material Contracts and Consents

The contracts listed on Schedule 11 are Material Contracts. Seller will use commercially reasonable efforts after closing to request counterparty consents for Material Contracts, but receipt of those consents is not a condition to Buyer's obligation to close.

## 12. Employee Matters

Buyer may decide which Seller employees receive offers. Any offers will be on terms determined by Buyer, and nothing in this Agreement creates an admission of successor liability or assumed employment obligations.

## 13. Restrictive Covenants

For 24 months after closing, Seller principals will not compete with the acquired SignalDesk product line in territories where Seller conducted that business during the 12 months before closing, subject to applicable law.

## 14. Closing Conditions

Buyer's obligation to close is conditioned on Seller's representations being true in all material respects, Seller's covenant compliance, receipt of required consents, and delivery of lien releases.

## 15. Disclosure Schedule Administration

A disclosure schedule qualifies only the specific representation or warranty that expressly references that schedule by number. No disclosure schedule qualifies any other representation or warranty unless that representation or warranty specifically references the schedule item.

## 16. Governing Law and Closing Logistics

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to conflict-of-law rules. The parties will sign and close this Agreement on the same date, and the simultaneous sign-and-close structure reflects the size and diligence posture of this transaction.
