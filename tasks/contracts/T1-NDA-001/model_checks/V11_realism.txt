Common in shorter NDAs, but a minor stylistic inconsistency next to the more expansive treatment given to other sections.

None of these rise to the level of "artificial" or broken drafting — they're minor organizational/style quirks, consistent with a real, professionally competent (if not perfectly polished) mutual NDA.
